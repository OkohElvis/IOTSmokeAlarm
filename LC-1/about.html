About the smart smoke alarm

Nothing is more important than your home safety. The Safe Option smoke alarm will advance your home security. When a fire first detected the alarm will be turned on. Our smoke alarm will also send an alert via the website so you can be aware of the fire wherever you are.

Features

Ever away from home? It doesnt matter how loud your smoke alarm is. Our smoke alarm bypasses that limitation. By sending an alert to you or your family members phone whenever smoke is detected, You can travel freely without worry. The smart smoke alarm is also effortless to set up. Simply put it in the room of your choice and log into your account. This alarm is easily used for all age groups.

Specifications

The alarm sends the message to your account instantly, The moment smoke is detected you will be alerted in less than a second. The smoke alarm requires a 5V power supply to run, so you won't have to worry about your alarm running out of batteries any time soon.

FAQ

Does the fact that it is a smart smoke alarm result in a shorter lifespan?
No, in fact the smart smoke alarm has a longer battery life span than traditional smoke alarms!
Does the distance of the home owner affect the alert the homeowner receives?
No the homeowner will be alerted regardless of where they are as long as they are logged into their account.
